JoyfulBody Therapy

JoyfulBody Therapy is a small business focusing on helping people get out of chronic pain using posture and muscle alignment. It's located in Alpharetta, Georgia and is run by one woman by the name of Jung Johnson. Jung helps people of all ages by giving them exercises to target dysfunctional areas of the body to bring it back to alignment. These exercises strengthen weaker parts of the body and relax tight areas of the body so that as a while, the body can function more efficiently. For her business, Jung was looking for some professional quality pictures of herself and of her business so that she could more effectively advertise it on social media and on her website.

Patient Connect Healthcare

Patient Connect Healthcare, also known as PCH, is a developing startup company passionate about helping people and their healthcare. They wanted their brand to break free of the negative stigma that healthcare and doctors in labcoats are scary, so their brand steers clear of any doctors in white coats, and instead focuses on feelings of cleanliness and minimalism. PCH is still under development, so it's brand guide is not accessible at this time.

Reset Robotics

GeorgiaFIRST Robotics

GeorgiaFIRST Robotics is a local nonprofit organization dedicated to helping spread FIRST's (For Inspiration of Science and Technology) mission across the globe. FIRST Robotics is the world's largest youth serving nonprofit advancing STEM education across the world, and currently has teams and competitions in over 80 countries. Here in Georgia, there are hundreds of teams across multiple grade levels that compete and volunteer at GeorgiaFIRST's events, all of them dedicated to the mission of furthering STEM education and access.

Client Type:

Nonprofit

Services:

2019 Annual Report, Photoshoot

Date:

November 4th, 2019 - March 13th, 2020

Project Link:

2019 Annual Report

Photo Gallery

GeorgiaFIRST's 2019 Annual Report was distributed to many event and program sponsors that are passionate about the cause GeorgiaFIRST represents. It was also distributed to many potential sponsors and partners as a means of helping introduce the company and it's employees to the workings of GeorgiaFIRST and how they could be involved. The photos were used for GeorgiaFIRST's social media, website, Annual Report, and more as a way to show others what GeorgiaFIRST is all about.
