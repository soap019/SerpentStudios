The Process

Many things in this process, such as the amount of time spent on each step, are dependent on your unique project. For some projects, like a photoshoot, the entire process may only take a few hours, whereas for a brand, it may take many weeks.

  • Step 1: Understanding

    Whatever your project may be, understanding exactly what you're looking for is key. Before we can start helping you and your business, we must first understand what problems you're looking to solve, who you're looking to target and what a successful execution looks like for you.

  • Step 2: Translation

    In step two, we look at a moodboard together so we can understand what exactly you're looking for in a visual sense. As a small business owner, we understand that you'll likely be involved from step 1, which is why in this step we work together in helping you understand what you're looking for and how we can execute on that for you.

  • Step 3: Tuning

    This step in the process is all about fine-tuning our creations to match what you're looking for. It's important for you to stand apart from your competition, but still blend into your market!

  • Step 4: Finalization

    Now that we have both figured out what you're looking for visually and how we can best execute on that, we now move into finalizing everything. This is the stage where we decide what stays, what goes, and what still needs to be changed before we stamp off on it and send it your way!
