Why do you need professional photos?

Hiring a photographer to take pictures of you and your business is an important next step to take in business. While everyone does have access to a smartphone and a camera, not everyone has the experience, direction and equipment to truly achieve a level of quality that sets you apart from your competition. High quality photos establishe trust with your customers, and tell them that your company has high standards and will treat them well.

The importance of:
Headshots

Headshots are needed for you and your team to convey professionalism in the workplace and beyond. Think of a nice headshot as a logo that represents you.

Product Photos

Professional product photos play a big part in influencing customers to buy - well lit and staged photos communicate professionalism and high quality.

Behind the Scenes

Behind the scenes photos capture you and your team hard at work on your business. It capture the passion, the dedication and thought that goes into everything you do.

How often should you be taking photos?

The needs for every business are different, but regardless of if you're selling a product, a service or some sort of craft, your business is always growing! You're gaining new team members, developing new products and continuously developing the back end of your business, and we're here to accomodate! Your monthly subscription of photography will look unique to you, but regardless of what it is you do, we will be there to help you capture the spark that goes into your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do I need to wear for headshots?

    It depends on what you want to use the headshots for. If they're for your company, they may prefer you dress a specific way or wear a uniform. Otherwise, when you come in for your headshots, business casual is usually the safest type, and neutrals or soft colors go along way for your complexion.

  • Can't I just take photos on my smartphone?

    Yes you absolutely can! But what you and your phone are lacking is years of photography experience, professional equipment, and a trained creative's eye. These are the real things you're paying for when you pay for someone to take pictures of you, your product and your business. Photography is not as simple a job as it may appear, and while it is easy to get started, high quality photos that convey your professionalism are a little bit more difficult to achieve.

  • How do I get my photos?

    After your session, whether it's for headshots, product photos or a behind the scenes shoot, you can expect your photos to be edited and sent to you within a couple days. You will receive a link to an album where all your photos are stored, and you can download them at your leisure! They will be fullsize JPG images so that you can print them or display them as large as you'd like.
