Frequently Asked Questions

  • What if I don't know what exactly I want?

    Don't worry, that's a very valid concern! Our job here is to hone in on what best fits your business. We do that by looking at your current market, the things your business does and the things you love about your business as well as a variety of other things. You'll still get a small sample of options to ponder over, and as with many things close to the heart, you'll know the right one when you see it.

  • What can I design?

    Anything you need. Whether that's something as simple as a flyer to post in your office, or as complex as a multimedia exhibit, we can help you piece things together and create whatever you might need for your business. Our team has a wide range of capabilities, so if you're not sure who might be the best person for your project, just ask us! We are happy to help with any new project.

  • Where can I use my designs?

    Anywhere you'd like. Whatever we make for you is for yours to own. In addition to the files you will be using, we will also be sending you the source files so you can make more adjustments later whenever you need. Use them on billboards, flyers, videos, etc.
